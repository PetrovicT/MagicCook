About us


The site is designed as a virtual notebook with recipes for our favorite dishes and desserts which are knocked us off our feet. And believe me, it is not easy to delight us. Our team consists of experienced chefs across 10 years of work experience. Over time, the blog has grown so much that we have a wide variety of dishes u offer, and vegetarian, lean or vegan, gluten-free .. and therefore can serve bigger number of people. The goal of writing recipes is to inspire you to cook as much as possible through recipes and stories, yes you will open to new tastes and to motivate you to make small changes and so little by little you improve your diet. We want to prove that nutritionally rich foods can be of course delicious and surprising, that such dishes can be made from available ingredients and not you must spend a fortune on a variety of fruit powders and exotic foods. Our diets are not one that can be described in one word, we eat all kinds groceries and we observe all recipes with critical eyes. We think it's important to know what it is in our plates, to try to cook as much as possible on our own and to keep the food we buy as good as possible more natural and as little processed as possible. We don't have to make world-famous dishes, no we must spend no more than half an hour a day in the kitchen, and that again is enough to feed us better.

We hope you enjoy and find something useful on this site!
